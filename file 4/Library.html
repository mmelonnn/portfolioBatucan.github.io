The Tiger's Whisker - Literature of Korea

In a Korean Hut in the 60's,
A soldier husband arrives home from the war much more unpredictable than ever.
His wife "Yun Ok", suspects he may have been bewitched.

Yun Ok gives in and visits the hermit
in order to create or brew the potion that Yun Ok's Husband needs,
Yun Ok requires a live Tiger's Whisker.

How will Yun Ok manage?
Yun Ok must learn the true meaning of bravery and sacrifice,
and realize that sometimes the things we desire most aren't always what we need.

With stunning visuals and heartwarming themes,
"The Tiger's Whisker" will take you on an epic adventure of courage, determination, and self-discovery.
